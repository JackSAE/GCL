Setup:

Add the prefab to your scene.
Drag your Background music into the Bgm Clips Array  


HowToUse:

Call: efxPlay and include an audio clip to play a single audio clip.
Call: bgmPlay and include an interger(equal to the song number) to play that background song.
call: efxRandomize and include an audio clip array to play a random audio clip at a random pitch.
call: MasterVolume and include a float to change the volume. This float has to be between 0 and 1.
